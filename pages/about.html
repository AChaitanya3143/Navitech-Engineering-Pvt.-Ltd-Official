Our Journey

14+ Years of Manufacturing Excellence

From humble beginnings to industry leadership—our story of growth, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality

2012

Foundation & Vision

Navitech Engineering established with a vision to revolutionize precision manufacturing in India. Started with a small team of 15 dedicated professionals and basic forging capabilities.

2015

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification, validating our commitment to quality management systems. Expanded capabilities to include precision casting and CNC machining operations.

2018

Major Facility Expansion

Doubled manufacturing capacity with state-of-the-art facility expansion. Introduced advanced sheet metal fabrication capabilities and automated quality inspection systems.

2021

Industry 4.0 Integration

Implemented cutting-edge Industry 4.0 technologies including IoT-enabled machinery, real-time production monitoring, and predictive maintenance systems for enhanced efficiency.

2024

International Expansion

Established strategic partnerships with global OEMs across automotive, construction, and industrial sectors. Achieved 500+ active client milestone with 99.8% quality rate.

2026

Leading the Future

Continuing our journey of innovation with advanced R&D capabilities, sustainable manufacturing practices, and commitment to being the trusted partner for mission-critical components.
